About Exodus Acupuncture and Chiropractic Centre

Established over Seven years ago at No 12 ajuwon Oluyole extension ibadan nigeria, Exodus Acupuncture and Chiropractic Centre has been a dedicated provider of specialized care, serving individuals from all walks of life.

Through our professional expertise and divine grace, we offer comprehensive treatment for partial spine injuries, paralysis, joint stiffness, chronic pain, arthritis, rheumatism, stroke recovery, and more. Our approach consistently yields positive outcomes for our patients.

"Half of your health depends on factors you can control (like diet, exercise), and half on things you can't (like genetics or environment)." - This motto guides our commitment to empowering you on your path to wellness.

WHICH AILMENTS CAN ACUPUNCTURE ADDRESS?

Acupuncture can help with issues like chronic pain, migraines, and stress. It encourages healing and boosts overall health.

Treatment frequency varies per individual. We tailor plans to support your health goals.

Yes! Combining these therapies can maximize benefits, offering comprehensive care for body and mind.
